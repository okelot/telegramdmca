# Telegram DMCA

`telegramdmca.com` is a focused SEO microsite built to capture high-intent search traffic around Telegram copyright infringement, leaked creator content, and Telegram DMCA takedowns.

The main business goal of this website is not to operate as a standalone brand destination. Its primary role is to attract relevant organic traffic and direct qualified leads to [Pellonia](https://www.pellonia.io/), which is the main service brand for copyright protection, anti-piracy support, and broader enforcement work.

## Core Strategy

This site is designed to support Pellonia in four ways:

1. Rank for Telegram-specific search terms that are commercially relevant.
2. Speak directly to creators, OnlyFans artists, agencies, educators, and digital businesses affected by Telegram piracy.
3. Build topic authority around Telegram DMCA, takedowns, leaked content, and copyright enforcement.
4. Funnel serious visitors to Pellonia through contextual calls to action and relevant backlinks.

## Audience

The current messaging is built for:

- Content creators
- OnlyFans artists and creator teams
- Agencies and managers
- Online educators and course sellers
- Premium content businesses
- Rights holders dealing with Telegram piracy

## How Pellonia Is Promoted

Pellonia is intentionally positioned as the primary conversion destination across the site.

This happens through:

- Repeated calls to action linking to `https://www.pellonia.io/`
- Contextual links to Pellonia's copyright service page
- Editorial backlinks to Pellonia blog posts about Telegram piracy and copyright enforcement
- Copy that frames Pellonia as the next step when manual DMCA reporting is not enough
- Supporting educational pages that warm up visitors before they click through

## Current Site Structure

- `index.html`
  Main commercial landing page targeting Telegram DMCA and takedown-service intent.

- `how-to-submit-a-telegram-dmca-takedown.html`
  Long-form educational page for informational search intent and creator-focused guidance.

- `telegram-dmca-faq.html`
  FAQ page targeting question-based searches and supporting topical depth.

- `styles.css`
  Shared visual system and orange theme styling.

- `script.js`
  Mobile navigation behavior.

- `robots.txt`
  Crawl instructions.

- `sitemap.xml`
  Sitemap for indexing support.

## Content Direction

The copy is intentionally written around:

- Telegram DMCA takedown service
- Telegram copyright infringement
- Leaked content removal
- Creator piracy
- OnlyFans content leaks
- Telegram channel and bot redistribution
- Anti-piracy support for digital products and subscriptions

The goal is to keep the content commercially useful while remaining substantive enough to support SEO and internal topical relevance.

## Backlinking to Pellonia

The site currently links to Pellonia pages that are closely related to Telegram copyright enforcement, including:

- Pellonia copyright protection
- Telegram DMCA and copyright protection articles
- Telegram anti-piracy guides
- Creator-focused copyright enforcement resources

These links are included to strengthen topical association and move users naturally toward Pellonia instead of treating outbound links as an afterthought.
